\section{Why Typesafe?}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Zero Configuration:} Powered by the \textit{Tectonic} engine, packages are automatically downloaded as you type.
    \item \textbf{Instant Preview:} High-performance PDFium rendering ensures your document looks exactly how you intend.
    \item \textbf{Smart Editing:} Context-aware autocompletion for environments, \texttt{\textbackslash cite} keys, and \texttt{\textbackslash ref} labels.
\end{itemize}
